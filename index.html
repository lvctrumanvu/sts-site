Vietnamese Catholic youth leadership

STS forms young leaders who live their faith and serve their community.

STS (Sau Thêm Sức) is a post-Confirmation journey that develops faith, leadership skills, and a spirit of service. Four schools share one mission: La Vang Morning, La Vang Afternoon, Mt. Pleasant Morning, Mt. Pleasant Afternoon.

Vision

Holistic formation: faith, skill, and a servant heart.

STS equips young people to become mature leaders who connect with the Church and the local Vietnamese community.

Deepen Faith

Scripture, liturgy, and prayer ground every leadership decision.

Build Skills

Communication, organization, and team facilitation for modern ministry.

Serve Community

Every project is a chance to live faith through action and love.
